Nixon's call to the moon


While they were on the surface of the moon they recieved a phone call at 23:48 EDT from the man who was the us president in the oval office at the time Richard Nixon he talked about what a proud moment this was for all of humanity and especially anyone american.

End of the moonwalk


Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin get back into the eagle and prepare to meet with the command module at around 01:11 EDT.

Crew Reunited


Neil armstron and Buzz aldrin got in the lunar module ascent stage and launched up towards the command module which was in orbit of the moon. They left behind the descent stage, a few experiments, cameras and the american flag which they planted on the surface of the moon. They brought with them rocks from the moon and film from their cameras. At 17:35 EDT the lunar module ascent stage docked with the command module and and so began their return to earth.
